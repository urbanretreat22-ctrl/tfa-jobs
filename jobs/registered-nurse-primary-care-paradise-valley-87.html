Description

The Registered Nurse is responsible for patient triage and education, assisting physicians during office hours and follow-up on prescriptions, procedures and tests. Maintain chart documentation to ensure all information about visit is completed and documented correctly.

Responsibilities • Adheres to the mission, purpose, philosophy, policies, and procedures of Tenet Physician Resources. • Maintains compliance with the Tenet HIPAA Compliance Plan, Privacy Standards, and Corporate Code of Conduct. • Assists in assessing patient needs by recording vital signs, history, medications, and chief complaints for physician review. • Performs diagnostic tests and prepares equipment for medical treatments, exams, and procedures as directed. • Collects and prepares lab specimens for transport, including completion of necessary forms and computer entry. • Administers prescribed oral medications and provides intramuscular and subcutaneous injections. • Triages patients to identify emergencies, providing immediate support and notifying providers of urgent medical needs. • Facilitates patient education, teaching, and the development of care protocols and instructional materials. • Compiles and analyzes medical and operational data to prepare reports for outcomes and program efficacy. • Ensures accurate data entry into Electronic Health Records and reviews treatment forms to maximize reimbursement.

Qualifications

Education: High school diploma/GED and completion of an accredited nursing program

Certification: Current State RN nursing license. Current CPR certification.

Experience: Must have a minimum of two years of clinical experience working in a physician office
