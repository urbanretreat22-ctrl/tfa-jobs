The City of Surprise is a fast-growing community where strong financial leadership is essential to turning plans into reality. As a Business Manager - Financial, you’ll play a central role in developing and managing department budgets, analyzing complex financial data, and supporting oversight of the development agreements and contracts that shape the City’s future. This role is ideal for someone with strong skills in Excel for purposes of reconciling data from multiple sources and translating complex financial information into clear deliverables. You’ll work in a collaborative, solutions-oriented environment where finance is a strategic partner. If you enjoy digging into details, improving processes, and using your technical skills to support responsible growth and high-quality public services, the City of Surprise offers the opportunity to make a visible and lasting impact. SUMMARY Incumbents are responsible for supervising staff, oversight and managing functions within their assigned areas with supervision from the Assistant Director. When assigned as the Business Manager - Financial, this includes completing and monitoring all financial transactions and Development Agreements; participating in department audits; preparing, monitoring, and analyzing Department budgets; analyzing operations of the various Department programs; managing grants, contracts and IGA’s; and preparing financial and budget impact analysis and statements regarding possible program and operational changes within the Department.&nbsp; EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE &nbsp; Bachelor’s Degree in a related field and five years of technical experience, including three years of supervisory or lead experience; or an equivalent combination of education and experience sufficient to successfully perform the essential duties of the job such as those listed above. &nbsp;Additional education cannot be substituted for experience.
