Company Description

Our Company assists with almost everything on the backside of the business, however it is primarily accounting, payroll and HR work. We will do software RFI/RFP/implementations, process improvements/writing SOPs, inventory control, annual reporting, setting up new entities, recruiting and soon our expansion of group events at our new property in the White Mountains. Our goal is to help our clients be successful and give them the peace of mind they need to sleep at night. We keep our ears open for pain points and jump in with some possible solutions to help. Referrals built our company, we do not advertise yet and we want to continue to grow due to our excellent reputation.

Role Description

The Accounting Consultant is a business partner to our clients. This position has flexible hours. It is Part-time to start and could move to Full-time if you like. It is mostly remote in the state of Arizona. Occasionally we need to go to a client to get them set up for remote work or if there is some sort of issue we cannot manage through screen-sharing, which is pretty rare. If you have excellent customer service, attention to detail, organizational and problem-solving skills, strong general ledger and cash/accrual accounting knowledge as well as a good understanding of how a business runs you should do very well in this position.
