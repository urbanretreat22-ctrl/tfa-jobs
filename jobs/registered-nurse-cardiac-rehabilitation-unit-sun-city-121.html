About the Role: In our 38-bed Cardiac Step Down unit, you will provide exceptional patient-focused care to those requiring telemetry monitoring and treatment for cardiovascular disorders, including cardiac arrhythmias and heart failure. You will care for patients recovering from myocardial infarctions, pacemaker implants, and other critical cardiovascular procedures, collaboratively working within an interdisciplinary team. Our unit, which accommodates approximately 50% progressive care patients, ensures comprehensive care throughout the inpatient stay, followed by personalized discharge planning to support outpatient needs.
