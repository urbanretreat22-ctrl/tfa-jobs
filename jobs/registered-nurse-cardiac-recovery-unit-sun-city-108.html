Responsibilities:

The Cardiac Recovery Unit is a 38-bed unit dedicated to providing the highest level of patient care for those requiring telemetry monitoring due to cardiovascular issues such as cardiac arrhythmias and heart failure. Our team cares for patients recovering from myocardial infarctions, coronary procedures, pacemaker placements, and electrophysiology interventions. With approximately 50% of our patients needing progressive care, we deliver quality care through a collaborative approach and create personalized discharge plans to meet outpatient needs.
