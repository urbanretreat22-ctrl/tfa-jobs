Responsibilities

Kforce's client, a publicly traded growing company is seeking a Senior Tax Associate This role can sit in Scottsdale, Arizona, or Princeton, NJ. The role will be 3 days in the office and 2 days remote. They will be responsible for supporting the Senior Manager for all tax planning activities, including US federal and state income tax, transaction tax return support, cash tax optimization, credits and incentives, and transaction diligence. Responsibilities include: • Calculate tax basis, 704(b) capital accounts, and transaction-related impacts while explaining the impacts for tax compliance • Preparation of memos and presentations to document diligence and transactions • Review of financial models, including cash taxes, partnership capital accounts, and credit and incentives related items • Assist with tax research for unique tax fact patterns • Collaborate closely with Accounting, Business Development, and Portfolio Finance to support transactions and ensure tax modeling accuracy • Drive process-change to optimize tax planning and enhance efficiency

Requirements • Bachelor's degree in Accounting, Finance, Business, Education, Taxation, Business/Administration, or similar • 3+ years of federal and state tax planning or compliance for partnerships with a public accounting firm or large corporate tax department; Experience with private equity or real estate tax is a plus • Partnership (Sub K) tax experience is a must • Experience drafting memos and presentations • Desire to learn data translation using Power BI, Alteryx, and Excel • Demonstrated ability to work cross-functionally and explain tax-related items to non-tax audiences • Strong analytical and Excel modeling skills, with proficiency in PowerPoint • Private equity or real estate tax is plus • Industry experience in energy, private equity, or real estate is a plus
