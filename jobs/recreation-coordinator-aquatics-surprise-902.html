The City of Surprise Parks &amp; Recreation Department is seeking a dynamic, motivated Aquatics professional to lead, elevate, and expand our swim lesson programs and water safety initiatives. We’re looking for someone who brings genuine enthusiasm for water safety, strong communication skills, and a commitment to creating exceptional learning experiences for swimmers of all ages. This position is ideal for someone who thrives in a high-volume, organized program environment, values collaboration, and takes pride in delivering high-quality aquatic programs to the community.&nbsp; A strong passion for aquatics and staff development is essential, along with the ability to strengthen learn-to-swim programs through thoughtful planning, effective scheduling, and comprehensive staff training. Join us in protecting our community and shaping a positive, educational, and fun aquatic environment. SUMMARY Incumbents provide skilled professional recreation programming for a geographic area, several specialty fields, or a major facility. &nbsp;Assignment may include providing Citywide programming in several specialty fields such as special events, aquatics, special interest classes, adaptive, youth and adult sports, tennis, youth council, civic engagement initiatives, and teens. &nbsp;Incumbents develop and coordinate a variety of Citywide classes, programs, and events for citizens of all ages; coordinate, implement, maintain, and evaluate programs; and may oversee facility operations. &nbsp;Responsibilities include preparing and coordinating the master schedule; staffing specific programs, including the recruitment of instructors and officials; and supervising part time staff and volunteers. &nbsp;Incumbents also perform administrative duties such as compiling data, maintaining program budget, preparing reports, soliciting bids, and providing quality customer service. EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE Bachelor's Degree in Recreation or a related field, and three years related experience, or an equivalent combination of education and experience sufficient to successfully perform the essential duties of the job such as those listed in the job description. CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS&nbsp; Must have at the time of hire and be able to maintain a valid Arizona Driver License. &nbsp; Positions that work in licensed programs require certification in Community Infant, Child, Adult CPR, and First Aid within six months of hire.
